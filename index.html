About

I'm Sneha Chandrashekhar, a WordPress Developer with experience in both freelance web development and corporate software engineering. I specialize in building custom WordPress websites tailored to business needs — from clean static sites to fully functional WooCommerce stores with advanced features. My work involves content management, performance optimization, and feature development to create fast, responsive, and user-friendly websites.

Before transitioning into web development, I worked as a Software Engineer at Quinnox, supporting large-scale applications using SYNON and RPG400 for clients across multiple global regions.

I enjoy solving problems, building clean digital experiences, and transforming ideas into functional, elegant web solutions.
When I’m not developing websites, you’ll often find me practicing yoga or hiking in nature.
